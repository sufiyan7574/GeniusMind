// FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents and students want to know before enrolling.

When do batches start for 2026–27?+
New batches for the 2026–27 academic year begin in [Month]. We recommend enrolling early as seats fill up quickly, especially for 11th and 12th Science batches.
Is there a registration fee to join?+
There is no registration fee. You only pay the monthly tuition fee after attending a free demo class and confirming your enrollment.
Can I attend a demo class before enrolling?+
Absolutely! We offer a free demo class for all programs. Fill out the enquiry form and our team will schedule a demo at your preferred batch timing.
What is the batch size per class?+
We maintain small batches of 15–20 students to ensure every student receives individual attention from the faculty.
What study material is provided?+
All students receive curated printed notes, practice worksheets, and previous year board exam question papers. This is included in the tuition fee at no extra cost.
How are parents kept informed about progress?+
We conduct monthly parent-teacher meetings and share performance reports after every test. Parents can also contact the faculty directly via WhatsApp for updates.
Are fees paid monthly or in advance?+
Fees can be paid monthly, quarterly, or for the full year. Discounts are available on quarterly and yearly payments. Sibling discount is also provided.
